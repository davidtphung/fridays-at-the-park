◆ The case for Coinbase

The Park is a music-and-art public good, and it already runs on Base.

No migration, no incentive to relocate, no speculative promise: 99% of a 56,000+ wallet community and 153,000+ mints are already native to Base. The Park has quietly become one of the most consistent onchain music engines on Coinbase's L2, releasing culture as a public good, funded by a self-sustaining DAO. Support here compounds an existing flywheel rather than betting on a new one.
